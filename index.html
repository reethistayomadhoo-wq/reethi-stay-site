Institutional Frameworks

Your Safety Is Our Absolute Priority.

Reethi Stay Omadhoo operates under full compliance with the Ministry of Tourism and Civil Aviation requirements. Led by our onsite manager Ali Adam, our property operates an actively maintained, comprehensive Disaster Management Framework.

🛡️

Complete Emergency Preparedness

Fully detailed operational protocols for fire, high winds, natural occurrences, and public safety. Dedicated 24/7 communications network, including dedicated guest mobile infrastructure distributed on check-in.

🧯

Fire Mitigation & Response Network

Equipped with localized Carbon Dioxide (CO₂), Water (H₂O), and ABC Dry Powder suppression systems spanning all primary structural zones. Strict bi-annual structural safety evaluation drills conducted systematically across all staff units.

🏥

Medical Evacuation Infrastructure

Direct line of communication and immediate first-responder access via the Omadhoo Island Health Centre. Fully codified trauma, cardiac, and basic first-aid operational framework linked straight to advanced medical transfer links in Malé.
